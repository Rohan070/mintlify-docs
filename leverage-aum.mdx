## How to Use AUM to Your Advantage

AUM is your **Enterprise Brain** - a powerful AI platform that runs completely on your own infrastructure, giving you full control over your data while providing enterprise-grade AI capabilities.

### Why AUM Gives You the Edge

**Complete Data Control**: Unlike cloud-based AI tools that expose your sensitive business data to external vendors, AUM runs entirely on your infrastructure. Your data never leaves your premises, eliminating the risks of data breaches, compliance violations, and loss of intellectual property control.

**Unlimited AI Power**: AUM works with the latest AI models (DeepSeek, Mistral, Llama3, and more) without any additional costs. You get enterprise-level AI capabilities without the unpredictable API costs and potential data leaks that come with cloud solutions.

**Seamless Integration**: AUM connects with your existing systems and workflows, allowing you to automate tasks across multiple applications. From creating documents and proposals to sending emails and writing in Notion, AUM works while you focus on what matters most.

**Industry-Specific Solutions**: Whether you're in law, healthcare, finance, government, or consulting, AUM adapts to your specific needs. It can analyze contracts, handle HIPAA-compliant patient data, process financial documents, or manage classified intelligence - all while maintaining the highest security standards.

### Key Advantages

- **Zero Cloud Risk**: Your sensitive data stays within your control
- **Compliance Ready**: Built for HIPAA, GDPR, SOC2, and other regulatory requirements
- **Offline Capability**: Works even in air-gapped environments or remote locations
- **Scalable Performance**: Handle millions of records and complex workflows
- **Cost Predictable**: No variable API costs or hidden fees
